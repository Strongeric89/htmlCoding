Application Description
Trello is a useful resource aimed at keeping track of progress made by people in the group.
Skype is a free and easy way to interact with your group or as a pair by being able to see and talk to each other. Also it is free to download.
Whatsapp is a free mobile application and useful way of telecommunications between groups.
This youtube link is useful link to tutorials on group work and any other videos that you require for group projects.
Not only does iMessage let you send free simple messaging service (SMS)-style and multimedia messaging service (MMS)-style messages, but also create and manage group messages, share location instantly, temporarily, or persistently, and more.

Communications is a key aspect when it comes to group work in college. It can be challenging for everyone who takes part but also very rewarding when it comes to end results on projects. It is all about working as a team and spread the work out equally. The team leader or "facilitator" is the one asigned to keeping an eye on all tasks, but ultimately it is down to "YOU" as an individual to contribute to the team.
